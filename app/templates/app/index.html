F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process to register a private limited company?

To register a private limited company, we handle everything from DSC, DIN, Name Approval, MOA/AOA drafting to MCA filing. The process usually takes 5–7 working days.

What are the annual compliance requirements for a Pvt Ltd company?

Annual compliances include filing MGT-7 (Annual Return), AOC-4 (Financials), Director KYC, maintaining board meeting minutes, and statutory registers.

Do you offer services for startups and MSMEs?

Yes, we provide end-to-end support including company registration, GST, MSME, IEC, brand name registration, and regular compliance advisory for startups.

What happens if ROC filing is not done on time?

Delayed filing attracts penalties ranging from ₹100/day to heavy fines depending on the form. It can also lead to disqualification of directors. Timely compliance is crucial.

Can I convert my sole proprietorship to a private limited company?

Yes, conversion is possible. We can help with forming a new company and transferring business assets, GST, and bank accounts accordingly.

Do you provide advisory or legal drafting services?

Yes, we assist with drafting board resolutions, shareholder agreements, MOUs, compliance reports, and offer virtual CS consultations for business decisions.
